Happy Deepavali wishes to all IPNU Members
 

Welcome to the Indian Students Association of Pusan National university, South Korea.

The Indian Students Association is one of the largest student organizations of the Pusan National University.We are a very active body, with our contributions benefiting both students of neighbouring universities and indians around Pusan. This is considered as the official indian community in PNU. This group was started on 1st july 2004, it was officially innaguarated by the president of external afairs on march 17th 2005. The main focus is to bring all Indians under one umbrella and to provide a platform to interact freely. We occasionally have get-togethers, tours, and cultural programs to make a feeling of unity here. We have been considered as one of the best cultural organizations in PNU. We promote our culture to anyone and everyone whom we are lucky enough to touch. Our vision provides the best explanation of our purpose.

.:Representing the Indian culture and promoting unity among its members and the community as a whole.

.:Providing opportunities for our members to cooperate, enjoy , maintain the spirit of nationality and the Indian community.

.:Interacting and learning from other organizations and having an influence in important campus issues.

.:Promoting awareness of our culture among our members and the other PNU communities.

Indian Students Association has always been an outstanding organization but with your ideas and participation, this year could be even better! .
